    SubReddit Predictor

    The Subreddit Predictor is for your every day reddit user. The purpose is to visit our site, type out a post, and then have the predictor return a list of the best places to post what they have written. This was a 5 day build with a large team that included two iOS engineers, three data scientists, two machine learning engineers, three front-end engineers, and one back-end engineer which was my role. My responsibilities were to establish a relational database using Node.js that could store users and save their posts, as well as handle authentication and full CRUD operations. This is amongst my favorite projects, as it truly exercised my ability to communicate efficiently in a large team.
